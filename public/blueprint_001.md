# Blueprint: TikTok Action Plan #001
**Source:** [Larry Agent Interview] - High Growth AI Automation
**Category:** Business Operations / Monetization

## 1. Summary
The "Larry Model" is a fully autonomous pipeline that generates and schedules high-engagement TikTok Photo Carousels. These carousels use a specific psychological formula (Conflict → AI Persuasion → Proof) to drive low-cost, high-velocity traffic to SaaS products or services.

---

## 2. The Core Formula
TikTok's algorithm currently prioritizes **slideshows** (carousels) over videos.
- **Engagement Multiplier:** 2.9x more comments, 1.9x more likes than video.
- **Structure:** 6 slides exactly (the engagement sweet spot).
- **Slide 1 (The Hook):** Focus on a human moment or conflict (e.g., "My landlord said no...").
- **Slides 2-5 (The Build):** Data, transformation, or "how it works."
- **Slide 6 (The Close):** Clear CTA (Link in Bio).

---

## 3. Technical Implementation (Atlas/Oracle)
### Phase A: Intelligence
- **Oracle (CFO):** Research 10-15 hooks at once based on competitor data.
- **Titan (Strategy):** Use high-reasoning models (DeepSeek-R1) to validate hook strength.

### Phase B: Asset Production
- **Image Gen:** Use Google **Nano Banana Pro** for photorealistic background assets with integrated text.
- **Locked Architecture:** Use a single, detailed base prompt for all 6 slides to ensure visual consistency.

### Phase C: Scheduling
- **Deployment:** Use an API-first tool (like **Postis**) to upload carousels as drafts.
- **Human Touch:** Manual 60-second step to add a "trending sound" and hit publish (API cannot add trending music yet).

---

## 4. Economic Projections
- **Cost:** ~$0.25 per post (API batching).
- **Revenue:** Case study reached **$588/mo MRR in 5 days**.
- **Efficiency:** 95% of work is autonomous.
